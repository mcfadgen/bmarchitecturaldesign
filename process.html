Our Process

Step 1:

Wishlist Formulation & Preliminary Design

Before we start designing your home we first meet with you get a better understanding of who you are and develop a wishlist that captures your needs and dreams. Once we have formulated this wishlist we then start preliminary design with includes:

  • Property Study
  • Zoning Study
  • Feasibility Study

Step 2:

Schematic Design

Once we have established your needs and wishlist along with the parameters of your property we then start to prepare design sketches, floor plans, elevations and 3D renderings. This is where we get creative and develop a design that is unique to you.

Step 3:

Technical Drawings

Technical drawings or permit drawings are developed next to coordinate with governing bodies such as municipalities and conservation authorities to obtain building permits necessary to commence construction. These drawings are also revised for construction and pricing.

Step 4:

Permit & Approvals

Our team is experience is acquiring all necessary permits and approvals, these include but are not limited to:

  • Building Permit
  • Conservation Authority Approval/Permit
  • Minor Variances
  • Heritage Building Approvals

Step 5:

Construction

Once we have received your building permit, it is time to break ground and build your dream home! We offer support throughout the construction of your home and make regular visits to ensure your project is being initiated as planned.
