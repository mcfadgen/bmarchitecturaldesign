Our Story

BM Architectural Design was founded and launched in 2015 as a one-man operation. Ben grew up with a natural interest in architecture and ability to sketch and create. As he started considering Architecture as his occupation he decided to spend his summers framing and building homes to better understand the ins and outs of construction and how architectural design is initiated. Eventually Ben studied and completed a Bachelor of Architect and Project Management.

In 2015, Ben decided to start BM Architectural Design. As demand for Ben’s designs and services grew so did his team, collection of services and his passion for design and architecture.

Today, BM Architectural Design is a well established Architectural Design firm that is reliable, Bauemler approved and services areas all over Ontario. BM Architectural Design has also expanded services and passion to include interior design and sustainable design services.

Why Choose BM Architectural Design?

“Building Society through Sustainable Design, Wellness, Function & Beauty.”

This motto and mission is the reason homeowners continue to reach out to us to design their most important and influential asset - their home.

  1. We understand that your home is more than a beautiful place, but it influences your wellness, functionality and the wellness of your surrounding environments.

  2. Great design does not just look appealing but it enhances our ability to live and find enjoyment everyday. Great design is considering the practical use of space and how it will function on a daily basis.

  3. We ensure that each home is customized to fit each client and the neighbourhood around it. For this reason we start every project with first understanding you, your lifestyle, your needs, your values, your wishlist and your style.

  4. At the end of each project, it’s most important that the home is a perfect fit for you and uniquely equipped for your needs.
